About Us!

Our Mission:
To provide the best quality clothing, shoes and accessories to our customers, whilst still keeping up good engagement with all ages, ranging from 14-75!

Our Vision:
To take over big sports brands and become the first single student website to be successful.

Our Values:
We value our customers and their satisfaction is our top priority.

Our Story:
SportifyHub was founded in 2024 due to a coursework a student in the UoB had to complete. This was the students first ever website, and after the coursework deadline, he decided to continue working on it, and later on it became an actual published website with real products!
